About

Capsule is a tool for planning capsule wardrobes.

Features:

  • Create an inventory of your closet.
  • Consider aquisitions and see how they fit into your current wardrobe.
  • Create capsule wardrobes around seasons, events, anything you'd like.
  • Save outfits for future reference.
  • Generate outfits based on your entire closet or a single capsule. Discover new ways to combine your pieces.

Why?

I wanted a tool to help me (1) organize my existing closet, (2) plan purchases, and (3) plan knitting and sewing projects that actually fit into my wardrobe.
