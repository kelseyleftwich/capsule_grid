About

This site is currently in development. If you have suggestions for features to be added or bugs to report please email me at kelsey.leftwich@gmail.com. Thanks!

Capsule is a tool for planning capsule wardrobes.

Features:

  • Create an inventory of your closet.
  • Create capsule wardrobes around seasons, events, anything you'd like.
  • Save outfits for future reference.
  • Generate outfits based on your entire closet or a single capsule. Discover new ways to combine your pieces.

 

Why?

I wanted a tool to help me (1) organize my existing closet, (2) plan purchases, and (3) plan knitting and sewing projects that actually fit into my wardrobe.

How does it work?

  1. Add articles of clothing.
  2. Create a capsule.
    • Enter target article type amounts (or don't).
    • Add articles to your capsule.
    • Make separate capsules for seasons, events, work/play, whatever!
  3. Generate random outfits. (Hit save if you like what it spits out!)
    • Generate from all articles or a specific capsule!
  4. Manually create outfits.
